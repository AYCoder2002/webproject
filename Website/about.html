What is CTB?

Founded in 2020 to help young people in north Manchester to express themselves musicially, with the help of pirate studios in Manchester, we have been able to help many artists to create music for free and express themselves to the wider world.

Want to be a part of us?

CTB takes talent from all over Manchester and brings that talent ot the front of the world! If you're confident enough - contact us today and we'll get you started rapidly!
